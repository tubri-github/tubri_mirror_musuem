Occasional Papers of the Tulane University Museum of Natural History

Henry L. Bart, Jr., Editor

Occasional Papers is a scientific publication of the Tulane University Museum of Natural History dedicated primarily to studies of systematics, natural history, biogeography, and environmental biology. It appears irregularly in consecutively numbered issues. Previously published issues are listed and available.
