Louis Agassiz

    Based on information in the

    catalog at the Museum of

    Comparative Zoology at

    Harvard University, Agassiz

    collected 9,342 lots and

    47,922 specimens. However,

    many of Agassiz’s specimens

    may have been discarded or

    given away before cataloging.

    Source: Karsten Hartel, MCZ
