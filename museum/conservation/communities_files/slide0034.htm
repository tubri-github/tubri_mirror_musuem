Concept of community assembly
and diversity is broadening
But biotic interactions tend to reduce the number of species
in communities.  What puts species in a habitat to begin with?
Regional/historical factors such as speciation and dispersal
add species to communities.
Community diversity reflects balance of local/contemporary
and regional/historical influences.
Traditionally held communities were saturated with species
(at equilibrium) due to competition and other interactions,
as if interactions were a force generating diversity.
