Non-governmental organizations
http://docs.lib.duke.edu/igo/guides/ngo/
World Bank defines NGOs as "private organizations that
pursue activities to relieve suffering, promote the interests
of the poor, protect the environment, provide basic social
services, or undertake community development"
Typically value-based organizations which depend, in whole
or in part, on grants,charitable donations and voluntary service.
Not directly affiliated with any national government, but often
have a significant impact on the social, economic and political
activity of the country or region involved.
Number in 10’s of thousands and cover all parts of the globe.
