Wilderness Society cont’d
•  Identifies, inventories and protect America's most fragile
  wild places.
•  Helped pass the Wilderness Act of 1964.   Former
  President, Howard Zahniser, wrote the Act.
•  Devotes expertise and leadership to coalition efforts,
  creating a broad base of support for conservation
  campaigns and reaching out to diverse communities.
•  75% of funding it receives used directly for conservation.
