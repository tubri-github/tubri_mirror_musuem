USGS/BRD Mission
BRD is a research focused agency.
BRD works cooperatively with Federal and State agencies
to share information necessary to develop a comprehensive
picture of the Nation's biological resources.
Majority of BRD programs and resources directed towards
needs of Interior resource management bureaus, such as
development of annual waterfowl regulations, research on
better land protection strategies for national parks, and
investigations on optimal water control practices (for
reservoirs) for enhancement of fisheries.
