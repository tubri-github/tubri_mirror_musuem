Other National Activities
Biomonitoring of Environmental Status and Trends (BEST)
A program designed to identify and understand the effects of
environmental contaminants on biological resources.
          
Bird Banding Laboratory (BBL)
The North American Bird Banding Program is jointly
administered by the USGS Biological Resources
Division and the Canadian Wildlife Service to study the
movement, survival, and behavior of birds.
          
Gap Analysis Program (GAP)
A geographic approach for assessing the current protection
of biological diversity over large geographic areas.
