Establishing a Health Surveillance Program for the Louisiana Gopher Tortoises
LNHP- LSU
The species has been severely reduced
in southern Alabama and Mississippi,
 southeastern Louisiana, southeastern
South Carolina, and Florida.
The prevalence of URTDS , pathogens and environmental toxins in the La population is unknown. Establish a health surveillance
program for the Louisiana’s
gopher tortoise  population will
help biologists and managers make good
management decisions for this threatened species.
