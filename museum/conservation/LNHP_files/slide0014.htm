RARE AND PROTECTED FISHES PROJECT
LNHP-Tulane University-ULM
•      The LNHP considers 27 fish species as species of concern due to their rarity or vulnerability. Two are presently listed as federally Threatened or Endangered (Gulf sturgeon, pallid sturgeon).  Knowing their status would allow the state to take conservation actions.
•
• Goal
•Update existing records in the LNHP database and survey additional sites in an attempt to add new records to the database.
•
• Approach
• Systematically visit streams known to have these rare or vulnerable fish species in the state.
