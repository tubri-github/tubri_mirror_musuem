Implementation of Red-Cockaded Woodpecker (RCW) Safe Harbor Program
•The Red-cockaded Woodpecker is indigenous to longleaf pine forest of southeastern United States. •Small, isolated, and genetically important groups do exist on private lands in Louisiana but their probability of existence is very low if these fragmented forest are not managed for the RCW. •RCW located on private lands have presented problems for landowners. •Certain management actions  are unlawful under the Endangered Species Act.
