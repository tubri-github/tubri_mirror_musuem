5-S planning approach
Systems
Focal conservation targets and their key ecological attributes.
Stresses
Most serious types of destruction or degradation affecting conservation
targets or key ecological attributes.
Sources of stress
Causes or agents of destruction or degradation.
Strategies
Full array of actions necessary to abate the threats or enhance viability of
conservation targets.
Success measures
Monitoring process for assessing progress in abating threats and improving
the biodiversity health of a conservation area.
