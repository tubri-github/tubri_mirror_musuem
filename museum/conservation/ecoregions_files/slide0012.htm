Importance of Public Lands
Public lands play an important role in the lives of Americans.
Provide a wealth of natural resources including minerals, coal,
oil, gas and timber; they are a source of clean air and water;
protect ecosystems; play a role in our country’s defense; and
offer places for outdoor recreation and spiritual renewal.
Certain public lands have been seriously impacted by overuse;
overwhelmed by the periodic influx of visitors. Learning the
hard way that the unregulated recreation of the past few
decades can have long-lasting negative impacts on the land.
