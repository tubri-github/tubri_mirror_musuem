Ecology and Conservation Biology
Science of ecology established early in 20th century.
Conservation Biology established only in last two decades.
Conservation Biology applies theories of ecology and
population genetics to management and conservation of
natural resources.
It is a young science and many of its precepts are
controversial; however, problems of loss of genetic
variability and biodiversity are such that consequences of
doing nothing outweigh risks of action based on unproven
hypotheses.
