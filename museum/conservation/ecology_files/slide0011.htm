Tolerance continued
Concept of tolerance is both environmental and genetic;
has fitness component with height of curve representing
both numbers (population) and genetic fitness.
Genetic variants that best suit individuals to local conditions
are promoted through natural selection.
Over the geographic range of a species, populations may
be differently adapted, contributing to genetic variation.
Migration (gene flow) among populations  increases genetic
variability within populations and likelihood populations will
survive in the event of environmental change.
