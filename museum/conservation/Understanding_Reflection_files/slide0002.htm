More than "touchy-feely"
Students and faculty usually think of reflection as "touchy-
feely" psycho-babble; approach with caution or resist
altogether.
However, reflection is simply a way of formalizing informal
learning; opportunity to reflect on and thus learn from
experience.
Takes many forms.  In this course you will participate in
in-class reflection sessions and will independently reflect on
your service in a journal.
