Natural Resource Conservation in theory and practice (EBIO 260)

Group Project Presentations

 

            During the last week of classes, students in the course will give group presentations based on their service work with their assigned conservation agency/organization.  The purpose of the presentations is to give everyone in the class an understanding of the full spectrum of service projects carried out during the semester and the conservation activities of various local conservation agencies/organizations from the perspective of students doing the service. The presentations should be formatted in Microsoft Powerpoint or comparable presentation software.
