Aquatic Habitat Research
Over 300 freshwater native fishes and over 200 anadromous
fish stocks are at risk.
FSR&D plays a key role in sustaining fisheries on FS lands.
Only research program in the country with a primary focus
on protecting, managing and restoring fish habitat.
Focus in on native salmonids and imperiled invertebrates
(e.g., mollusks, crayfish).
