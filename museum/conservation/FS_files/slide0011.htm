Atmospheric Sciences Research
Ozone and other atmospheric contaminants can impair the
growth of trees and forests. Air pollution and rainwater
contamination can also change the biological structure of
forests and rangelands. Solar radiation (e.g., increases in
ultraviolet radiation) can also harm forests.
FSR&D atmospheric science research focuses on effects
of air pollution, weather and climate on ecosystem
function and productivity on public lands.
Examines effects of weather on forest fires.
