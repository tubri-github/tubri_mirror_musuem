Wildlife Habitat Research
360 T&E species are associated with National Forests
and Grasslands. Another 2,500 species are categorized by
Regional Foresters as 'sensitive’.
FSR&D provides land managers with technical information
for sustaining plant and animal populations by protecting,
managing, and restoring high quality habitat.
Particular emphasis is placed on threatened and endangered
species and on declining or vulnerable communities such as
Neotropical migratory birds, old-growth forests and riparian
communities.
