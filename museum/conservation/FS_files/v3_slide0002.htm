Established in 1964 by Wilderness Preservation Act
Forest Service is one of four federal agencies involved
in managing wilderness areas in the U.S.
Wilderness Areas protected by law as places “where
earth and its community of life are untrammeled by
man, where man himself is a visitor who does not
remain” and “leaves no trace”.
4.41% of the continental United States is protected as
Wilderness (106 million acres).  FS manages 1/3 of this
land.
