Service Learning
Service learning is a required component of this course.  Students
must enroll in one-credit of service learning (EEOB 389).
Students must complete a minimum of 40 hours of service with a
local natural resource agency/organization.
Students must keep a journal to document service activities.
Journals will be collected and graded at the end of the
semester.
Students will work in groups on service-related research
projects and will give presentations based on their research.
One hour of class per week will be devoted to reflecting on service
activities and discussing how these activities relate to lecture topics.
