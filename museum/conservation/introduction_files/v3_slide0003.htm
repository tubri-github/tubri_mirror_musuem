Course Overview and Objectives
Q. Who is responsible for conserving natural resources?
A. In the United States, conservation entrusted to agencies of
state and federal government and an increasing number of non-
governmental organizations (The Nature Conservancy, Sierra Club,
Audubon Society).
Q. What principles guide these organizations?
A1. Ideally, scientifically-established and ecologically-sound principles.
