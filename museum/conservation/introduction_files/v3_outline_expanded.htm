Case in point: US Fish and Wildlife Service’s
Office of Endangered Species

Problems with listing process
In order to be protected by the Endangered Species Act, a species must first be “listed”.
Getting a species on the list can take years, and is frequently challenged by pro-development interests.
USFWS presently is observing a politically mandated moratorium on new listings.
