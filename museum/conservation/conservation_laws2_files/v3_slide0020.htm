Ecosystem approach
More appropriate to protect overall ecosystems than single
species.
Emphasize importance of protecting ecosystems before
threats reach critical level (avoid brinksmanship).
Replace CBA with “Safe Minimum Standards”, which
establish the minimal level of habitat or population needed
to protect species/ecosystems.
Respect “basic rights”; seek middle ground in
promoting protections; garner local support; give residents
voice in process /ownership of conservation ideals.
