Problems with ESA
ESA gave the Fish and Wildlife Service absolute authority
in protecting species; was in many ways too powerful.
Halted projects supported by powerful people…
Amended in 1978 to allow oversight committee to waive
regulations under special economic circumstances;
allowed exemptions when critical habitat designations
were judged too costly.
In minds of environmentalists, amended act gave humans
and development interests the upper hand in endangered
species conflicts.
