Conservation easements
Efforts to avoid conflicts over private property rights have
led to other conservation initiatives at the state and local
levels.  One such  initiative is conservation easements.
Landowners agree with conservation agency or NGO to
restrict certain kinds of activity on land in order to achieve
some conservation objective.
Restrictions lower the assessed value of the land and hence
the property/inheritance taxes.
CE’s non-confrontational and focused on broader
conservation objectives than ESA. Hugely popular; 1.4
million acres in CE’s as of 1998.
