NEPA
Signed into law with little fanfare in 1970, ushering in
“decade of the environment”.
Full of vague platitudes: “continuing responsibility of
the federal government to use all practicable means…
to fulfill responsibilities of each generation as trustee
of the environment for future generations, assure…
esthetically and culturally pleasing surroundings,…
preserve important, historic, cultural, and natural
aspects of our natural heritage…”
But buried in the law were two requirements that
would give it “teeth”.
