Natural Communities Conservation
California implemented program of Natural Communities
Conservation Planning to protect the California
gnatcatcher, which was losing its coastal scrub habitat to
residential development.
Feared that designating the species as endangered would
cause dissention; imperil the species even more.
Established voluntary scrub habitat reserves designed by
land owners themselves; subject to scientific review.
Landowners involved in planning process from the outset;
built-in flexibility; too early to say how effective it will be.
