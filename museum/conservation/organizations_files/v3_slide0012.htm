Dept. of Interior’s
Land Management
DOI manages 507 million acres of
surface land, or about one-fifth of
the land in the United States,
including:
•  262 million acres by Bureau of Land Management
•  95 million acres by the Fish and Wildlife Service
•  84 million acres by the National Park Service
•  8.6 million acres by the Bureau of Reclamation
  associated with reclamation projects
•  56 million acres by the Bureau of Indian Affairs
