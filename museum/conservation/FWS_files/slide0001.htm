U.S. Fish and
Wildlife Service
Programs are among the oldest in the world dedicated to
conservation of natural resources
Traces its origins to the U.S. Commission on Fish and
Fisheries in the Department of Commerce and the
Division of Economic Ornithology and Mammalogy in
the Department of Agriculture.
Manages the 93 million-acre National Wildlife Refuge
System with more than 520 National Wildlife Refuges
and thousands of small wetlands and other special
management areas.
