Orectolobiformes, or the carpet sharks, have the following characteristics:
•5 gill slits, with the last two overlapping
•Specialized nasal connections to the mouth corners
•Oviparous, or ovoviviparous
•Dermal flaps in the head region of the wobbegongs
•Located in warm waters, limited to a marine environment, they can be found in shallow to deep waters.
•Their name derives from the upper lobe of the tail, which is both aligned to the body, and larger then the often reduced lower lobe (most species but not all)
