Lamniformes, or the Mackerel sharks, have the following characteristics:
•Absence of Nictitating membrane
•5 gill slits
•Ovoviviparous
•Located in cold to warm waters, limited to a marine environment, they can be found in coastal waters or open ocean
•They have unique teeth arrangement, which is set up in the following way: large anterior – small intermediate – large lateral – small posterior.
