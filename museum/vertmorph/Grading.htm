Grading:

Your final course grade will be based on the percentage of 800 points earned in the following areas. You will take two lecture exams worth 100 points each and a comprehensive final exam worth 200 points for a total of 400 lecture points; four lab practicals worth 100 points each for a total of 400 lab points. You will have the option of doing a special lab project and substituting the score for one of your practical lab scores. This will be discussed in lab.  Final letter grades will be assigned base on the percentage of points you earn, according to the following scale:      

92-100% = A

90-91% = A-

88-89% = B+

82-87% = B

80-81% = B-

78-79% = C+

72-77% = C

70-71% = C-

68-69% = D+

60-67% = D

58-59% = D-

< 58% = F
