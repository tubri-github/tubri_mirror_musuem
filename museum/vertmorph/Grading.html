Grading:

Final course grade will be based 50% on lecture and 50% on lab. Students will take two lecture exams worth 100 points each and a comprehensive final exam worth 200 points for a total of 400 lecture points; students will also take four lab practicals worth 100 points each for a total of 400 lab points. Letter grades will be assigned according to a strict point scale as follows (no curve):

716-800 pts = A

636-715 pts = B

556-635 pts = C

480-555 pts = D

< 480 pts = F
