Aquatic Insects
Phylum Arthropoda
   Class Insecta/Hexapoda
•  Most well studied group of benthic macroinvertebrates,
taxanomically and ecologically.
•  Frequently the most abundant large organisms collected
during benthic sampling. = Large biomass.
• 13 Orders of insects in North America, only 5 Orders are
strictly aquatic (3%-4% total spp.).
