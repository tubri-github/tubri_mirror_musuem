    River Continuum and Serial Discontinuity Concepts

    River Continuum Concept:
    • physical variation in river from headwaters to mouth consists of a inter-related gradient.
    • “continuum of biotic adjustments”

    Serial Discontinuity Concept:
    • Regulated streams are seen as “large-scale experimental systems” with disruptions in continuum gradients.
