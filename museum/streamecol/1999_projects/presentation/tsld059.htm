    Discussion

    In many instances, there did appear to be trends to changes in community structure at the family and species levels; however, our study could have sources of error.
    • Less complete data set for pre-impounded collections.
    • Methods for collecting data are not known.
    • Subsampling variability
    • Lack of material to support observations on families; more resources are needed.
    • More accurate models would examine at the genus or species levels; generalizations concerning families are often too broad.
