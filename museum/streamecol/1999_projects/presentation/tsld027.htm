    Nitrogen

    Comparison

  • Organic Nitrogen is the most common form in both rivers.
  • Nitrate, nitrite and ammonium inputs are fairly steady in both streams.
  • A general increase in nitrogen concentrations is seen in the Pascagoula River, but is not statistically significant.
  • Nitrogen concentrations on the Pearl River are fairly constant without any significant changes.
  • The concentrations are not significantly different between the two rivers.
