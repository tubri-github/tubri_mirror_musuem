    Comparison of Fish Species Composition Before and After Construction of the Ross Barnett Reservoir

    In order to gain a better understanding of the specific effects the Ross Barnett Reservoir had on the fish populations in the Pearl River, data from pre- and post-impoundment collections was analyzed. More extensive data was available for the post-impoundment period; therefore, the initial set was subsampled to get a more balanced comparison.
  • Pre-Impoundment Collections: 1950-1963
  • Post-Impoundment Collections: 1965-1978
