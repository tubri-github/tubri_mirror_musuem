    Poeciliidae (Livebearers): More in post-impoundment.
  • Diversity of habitats suggests the family is highly tolerant and adaptive.
  • Skilled colonizers, and a single female can colonize a new population
  • They can occupy “fringe habitats” which are often harsh and unstable environments where few other fish can survive.
  • Eurythermal and euryhaline (Gambusia affinis, the western mosquitofish which appeared in the records, can survive in salinities ranging from fresh to sea water.
