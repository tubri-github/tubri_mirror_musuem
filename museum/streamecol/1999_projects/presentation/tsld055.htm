    Cyprinidae (Carps and Minnows): Greater in post- samples.
  • Large, diverse, and very speciose
  • Habitats include swamps, springs, streams, lakes, reservoirs, and large flowing rivers (fill in nitches?)
  • Hybognathus nuchalis (Mississippi silvery minnow), a fish collected in our post- impoundment samples, is said to be abundant where streams meet large rivers and impoundments, indicating a preference for flowing conditions (Fingerman and Suttkus 1961).
