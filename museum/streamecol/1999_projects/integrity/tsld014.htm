Specimen Identification

  • All samples were identified by different lab groups for every site

  • Organisms were keyed out usually from the level of a known order for macroinvertebrates and a known family for fish

  • Freshwater Fishes of Louisiana by Douglas (1974) was used to ID fish by the various groups

  • Ecology and Classification of North American Freshwater Invertebrates by Thorp and Covich (1991) and An Introduction to the Aquatic Insects of North America by Merritt and Cummins (1996) were used to ID macroinvertebrates by the each groups
