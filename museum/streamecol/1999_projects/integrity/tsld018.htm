Calculation of %EPT

  • Percentage calculated from proportion of individuals from the orders Ephemeroptera, Plecoptera, and Trichoptera (Hauer 1996)

  • The higher the percentage, the better the integrity

  • Advantageous because identification does not have to be very precise

  • Disadvantageous because it does not take into account the other macroinvertebrates sampled
