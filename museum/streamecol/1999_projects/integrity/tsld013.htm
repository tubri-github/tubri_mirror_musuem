Specimen Collection

  • In the case of the Arkansas streams, sampling was performed on separate sections, this was superfluous because all the samples were pooled for IBI and EPT analyses

  • Fish were sampled using an electroshocker and sieve nets within netted off areas along the stream

  • Except in the case of South Fork Caddo, fish specimens were taken from the entire stream reach (s) being analyzed

  • Fish samples were placed in ethanol for preservation

  • Macroinvertebrates were sampled using dip nets and Surber techniques

  • The invertebrates were also placed in ethanol for preservation purposes
