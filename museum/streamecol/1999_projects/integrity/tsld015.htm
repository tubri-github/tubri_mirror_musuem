Calculation of IBI

  • All analyses done following the techniques utilized by Karr et. al. (1986) for an Illinois stream

  • various metrics were selected based on organism sensitivity to human disturbance

  • Each metric is given a point value of 1, 3, or 5 depending on the actual number or overall percentage of the indicated organisms within the sample

  • The higher the tabulated IBI score, the more pristine the stream

  • This rating system is based on the species composition found in an undisturbed stream preferably in a similar habitat to the stream being analyzed
