Comparative Analysis

  • Compared the data and rated the five streams with respect to each other.

  • Each stream was ranked in value 1-5. Then tallied to get a final score which categorized the streams from most to least altered.

  • Proportion of Green Sunfish, # of diseased individuals, and # of hybrids were omitted.

  • Again, the results were compared to the proportion of the watershed forested, to estimate the effect of anthropogenic alterations on the watershed.
