Significance of Metrics

  • Metric

  • Total number of fish species

  • # of darter species

  • # of sunfish species

  • #of sucker species

  • #of intolerant species

  • Significance

  • the more fish, the better the integrity

  • sensitive to degradation due to their specificity for reproducing and feeding in benthic habitats (siltation and oxygen content effected by disturbance)

  • effected by degradation of pool habitats and instream cover

  • intolerant to habitat and chemical degradation

  • pretty obvious, but too many species in this category it reduces the usefulness of IBI
