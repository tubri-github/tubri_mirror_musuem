Significance of Metrics (continued)

  • Metric

  • proportion of green sunfish

  • proportion of omnivores

  • proportion of insectivorous cyprinids

  • proportion of piscivores

  • #of individuals in sample

  • proportion of hybrids

  • proportion with disease,tumors, anomalies

  • Significance

  • green sunfish increase in abundance in degraded streams, they are very tolerant

  • because of their generalized diet, omnivores tend to increase in # in degraded streams

  • #s decrease with degradation due to variability in insect populations

  • higher #s indicate a healthy, trophically diverse community

  • the less individuals, the more degraded the site

  • degradation may alter reproductive isolation of species

  • any of these things is NOT a good sign
