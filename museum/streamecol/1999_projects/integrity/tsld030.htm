Why did IBI and %EPT give different results?

  • There were skewed results because it was easier to find Macroinvertebrates in LA than in AK

  • A large rain event took place after a long period of low flow which may have contributed to low macro populations

  • Some areas of streams were easier to sample from than others (e.g. depth of water, strength of current, etc.)

  • Different people sampling led to slight variations in methods used and what organisms were collected

  • Greater numbers of fish and macros collected at some streams than at others

  • There may not be a direct correlation between IBI and EPT
