    Regulation of stream fish assemblages may be stochastic (driven by response of species to environmental conditions) or deterministic (driven by predation and competition) (Poff and Ward 1989).
    Matthews (1986) found fish fauna to be stable and persistent across years. Species richness and abundance was recovered eight months after severe flooding of Arkansas stream. However, he states:
    • Floods can have more serious effects during reproductive season than any other time of year.
    • Restoration after floods depends on:
      - adult survival and redispersal
        - high recruitment of young after the event
