Data Collection Methodology

  • Seven streams were selected from the southeastern U.S. Gulf Coast (between 30-32N & 91-87W) where at least 50 years of data was available.

  • Daily discharge was collected from United States Geologic Survey (USGS) for the years of 1988 to 1997.

  • Daily, monthly, and annual discharge was collected from the Hydro Climatic Data Network (HCDN) for the years of 1931 to 1987.

  • USGS data was broken down into monthly and annual discharge.

  • All data was then merged.
