  • In study comparing natural (low flow flux) vs. regulated (high flow flux) streams, smaller slow current fishes (bluegill, small mouth bass, tasselated darter) could not persist in microhabitats with increased flux, as could larger, midstream species (large mouth bass, white sucker) (Bain, Finn, and Brooke 1988).
