Claiborne Lock and Dam

  • Located at river mile 117.5

  • Opened 1969

  • Operated and owned by the US Corps of Engineers

  • Contains six 60ft gates in the river channel

  • Not used for hydropower

  • Forms the Claiborne Lake- creates 9-x-200 ft navigational channel, average pool elevation of 35 ngvd

  • Lake is 60 miles long

  • Lake is consider more rustic of the three, also used for water sports

  • Total tonnage of shipments during 1998=> 398,205 tons (compare to Mississippi=> 76,814,256 tons)
