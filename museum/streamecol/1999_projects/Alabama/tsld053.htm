Basic Effects

  • Create large lentic environment (lake), where there used to be a lotic environment (river)

  • The natural cyclic flow and flooding of the river is restricted

  • Decreases turbidity in some areas while greatly increasing it in others

  • Oxygen depletion

  • Thermal and chemical stratification

  • Retaining of pollutants behind dams
