Statistical Analysis of Discharge and Stage Data for the Red, Alabama, and Pascagoula Rivers

  • Discharge data from the Army Corps of Engineers and United States Geological Survey was collected for the three rivers.

  • The data was converted from its text format to excel where it could be processed and analyzed.

  • Pre- and Post construction periods were developed consisting of five year periods, where we felt that the construction of the lock had little to no effect on the discharge values.

  • Monthly and Annual discharges were calculated from the daily discharge data.

  • Stage data for the Red River was compiled and processed in the same manner.

  • The processed data was then ready to be graphed and analyzed.
