The Pascagoula

  • Formed by the joining of the Chickasawhay and the Leaf Rivers

  • Flows southward 80 miles to the Gulf of Mexico

  • Mostly natural water way- no impoundments

  • Daily water use- 3.3 million gallons for irrigation, 4.2 million gallons for livestock, and 49 million gallons for industry

  • Water quality is usually good to excellent, only localized contamination problems
