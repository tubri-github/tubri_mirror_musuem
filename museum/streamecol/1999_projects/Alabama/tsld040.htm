Millers Ferry Lock and Dam

  • Located at river mile 178

  • Opened 1969

  • Operated and owned by the US Corps of Engineers

  • Contains seventeen 50 ft. gates in the river channel

  • Utilized for hydropower- three 25,000 kilowatt units (serves 50,000+ homes)

  • Forms the William Dannelly Lake- creates 9-x-200 foot navigational channel, average pool elevation of 20 ngvd

  • Lake is 103 miles long

  • Lake is used for recreational water sports
