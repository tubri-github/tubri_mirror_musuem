Robert F. Henry Lock and Dam

  • Located at river mile 281

  • Opened 1972

  • Operated and Owned by US Corps of Engineers

  • Contains eleven 50 ft. gates in the river channel

  • Utilized for hydropower- four 17,000 kilowatt units (serves 45,000+ homes)

  • Forms the R.E. Woodruff Lake- creates a 9-x-200 foot navigational channel, average pool elevation of 125 national geodetic vertical datum

  • Lake is 77.4 miles long

  • Lake is used for recreational water sports
