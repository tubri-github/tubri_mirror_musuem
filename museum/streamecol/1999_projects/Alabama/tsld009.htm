The Red River

  • Slow, meandering stream characterized by high siltation, dynamic bank formation

  • headwaters in Salt Fork and North Fork Rivers, New Mexico

  • Flows SE through Texas, Oklahoma, Arkansas, and Louisiana for 1,360 miles

  • Naturally flows via Old River to both the Mississippi and Achafalaya Rivers

  • Mid-century, Red River was diverted completely to the Achafalaya near Simmesport, LA

  • Navigable to Shreveport- during high flow navigable to Fulton, AR

  • Drains an area of 91,400 square miles
