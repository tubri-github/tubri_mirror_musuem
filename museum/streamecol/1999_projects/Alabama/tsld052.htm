Results

  • The discharge data for both rivers show higher discharge after the construction of the navigation lock.

  • The increase in the discharge of the Red River was much greater post-construction of the Overton dam than the increase seen on the Alabama River post-construction.

  • The discharge of both rivers showed the same seasonal variability before and after the construction of the lock.

  • The Red River stage height data showed little variation post-construction of the Overton Lock, although discharge data continued to show the same variation as prior to the construction of the lock.

  • We were not able to access the historical stage data for the Claiborne Lock, but it would most likely show similar effects after the construction of the lock.

  • The Alabama River and the the Pascagoula River show similar seasonal variations in discharge.
