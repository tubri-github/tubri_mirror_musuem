BASINS
  • Objectives and Components
26
BASINS addresses three objectives:
  • To provide an integrated watershed and modeling framework.
  • To support analysis of point and non-point source management alternatives.
  • facilitate examination of environmental information.
27
Evolving beyond just creating TMDL analysis, BASINS has been utilized to evaluate:
  • Urban/rural land-use
  • Habitat management practices
  • Storm water management issues
  • Drinking water source protection measures
  • Farming practices (crop,animal)
