Non-Point-Source (NPS) Pollution
Introduction of impurities into a surface water body
or aquifer through a non-direct route and from sources that are “diffuse” in nature
•Usually intermittent
•Difficult to identify, isolate and control
NPS  agricultural impacts:
•leading source of water quality impacts to surveyed rivers and lakes
•third largest source of impairments to surveyed estuaries
•major contributor to ground water contamination
•major contributor to wetland degradation
