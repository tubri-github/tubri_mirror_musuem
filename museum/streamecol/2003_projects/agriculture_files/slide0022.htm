Riparian forest buffers (RFBs) - managed ecosystems to intercept runoff from fields before it reached a stream.
Zone 1 -permanent forest vegetation next to stream.
Zone 2 -area managed to control pollution in subsurface flow and surface runoff by transformation, storage, infiltration or deposit.
Zone 3 -grass or other herbacious filter strip to change concentrated flow to sheet flow and to remove sediment and its associated pollution.
