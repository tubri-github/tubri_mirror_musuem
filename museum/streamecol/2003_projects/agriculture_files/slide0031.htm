What about Sediment?
•Sediment can effect invert’s in a few ways!
–Sediment can change substrate, therefore altering their habitat.
–Sediment can block out sun affecting the algal community and therein affecting the invert’s that eat algae.
–Physical abrasion of body surfaces
•Increases susceptibility to infection
–Filter feeding of macroinvertebrates
•These changes can not only effect populations but can also effect the invertebrate diversity!
