•  Life cycles of aquatic insects can vary from a total life cycle
of two weeks to 4-5 years.
•  Giant Stoneflies and Megaloptrans often in larval stage for
several years.
•  Most mayflies in larval stage one year. Emerge, mate, and die
after several hours.
•  Most aquatic insects have annual life cycles (like Mayflies)
Typical life cycle
•  Eggs deposited
•  Eggs hatch soon after
•  Larvae grow slowly, many molts (instars)
•  Reach maturity about one year later
•  Emerge, mate and lay more eggs
