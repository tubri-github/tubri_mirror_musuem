Travel, Visas, Passport, and Currency: You are responsible for all travel arrangements, including procurement of your own passport, visas, and acquisition of proper currency sufficient for your stay.

Passport: Your travel to South Africa will require a passport from your country of residence. This can take several weeks to obtain without fees for expedited processing. Thus, you will need to process this immediately.

Visas: Visa information can be found here: http://www.southafrica.info/travel/documents/visas.htm
