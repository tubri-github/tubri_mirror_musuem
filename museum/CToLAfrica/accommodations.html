Excursions
The venue for the workshop will be Rhodes University in Grahamstown.  Participants are recommended to stay at the Graham Hotel, 123 High Street, Grahamstown, which is within walking distance of Rhodes University and close to SAIAB .  If you prefer accommodations at a guest house (Bed & Breakfast), our hosts at SAIAB recommend the High Corner guest house or 137 High Street guest house both of which are also close to Rhodes and the Graham Hotel.   A complete list of hotels and guest houses in the area can be downloaded at the link below for more adventurous participants.   However, reimbursement for lodging expenses is limited to 500 ZAR per night for participants supported on workshop funds.
