Described Catostomus cyprinus from the Elk River and streams entering Chesapeake Bay. Rafinesque (1820) included Le Sueur's Catostomus cyprinus under subgenus Carpiodes. Finally, in 1854, Agassiz elevated Carpiodes to generic level.
