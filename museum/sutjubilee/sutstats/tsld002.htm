Tulane Fish Collection

    With 191,195 lots and 7,120,528

    million specimens, the Tulane

    Fish Collection is one of the

    largest collections of post-larval

    fishes in the world. In 1990, it

    was the 6th largest collection in

    North American in terms of lot

    numbers and the third largest in

    terms of total specimens. It is

    one of only four National Center

    of Ichthyology Resource

    Collections in North America.
