Long term surveys

    What sets the Tulane Fish

    Collection apart from other fish

    collections in North America is

    the large proportion of material

    in the collection from long-

    term, environmental monitoring

    surveys. The surveys were

    initiated by Suttkus and the late

    Gerald Gunning. The longest

    running surveys (Red, Pearl and

    Alabama rivers) are still ongoing.
