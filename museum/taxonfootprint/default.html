Taxon Footprint Validation of Georeferenced Localities


Taxonomic footprints, as applied to georeferencing, describe areas of occurrence (or co-occurrence for multiple species) that when visualized can be used to inform how well the results of georeferencing fit within the knowledge of existing point occurrence data.



Try out a demo here.
