Last Fall we were collecting carpsuckers with John Lyons in the Wisconsin River. We were aware that there might be more than one morphological form of quillback in the Upper Mississippi River (deeper and narrow bodied forms). On this trip we indeed collected two morphological forms of quillback. We pulled some tissues and felt that it would be a worthwhile opportunity to investigate this problem further using molecular techniques.
