Data Analysis

  1. Data were evaluated for ambiguities and "clear" sequence length using Factura 1.2.Or6 (Applied Biosystems, Inc.)
  2. Multiple simultaneous alignments of sequences using Gene Works 2.45 (Oxford Biomolecular)
  3. PAUP 3.1:Exhaustive search, computed 50% majority rule, weighted/unweighted
  4. Morphometrics: Measurements of head and body proportions were made to assess their importance in distinguishing populations of C. cyprinus
